Background

Born Asriel Annette Rapture Rostadottir, she gained her middle name Miku from her family of dragons. While there isn't much information on what her life was like before the age of 10, she was found by an ice dragon named Fir who, with the help of other dragons, not only saved her life but helped her thrive on the mountain.
